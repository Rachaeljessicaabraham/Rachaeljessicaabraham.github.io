RAINWATER HARVESTING

In keeping with our principle, of utilizing every precious drop of water we will be having rooftop Rainwater harvesting & create underground storage tanks which will be a mandatory requirement for every house in the village.

WASTE WATER MANAGEMENT

Every household in the Ecovillage will have its own Grey&Black water recycling system based on the 3-5 stage natural filtration process.. Residents are also encouraged to install domestic grey water recycling.

WATER MANAGEMENT

Our integrated approach to water management systems & Practices includes Reforestration,Rainwater harvesting, Ground water recharge systems, Grey& Black water treatment ,Natural earth water storage systems all this will ensure that every drop of water is responsibly used towards fulfilling the need of the hour & our vision of leaving this priceless & scarce resource for our children. As part of our CSR initiatives we are educating & implementing new self sustaining water management practices for our neighboring villages.

BIODYNAMIC FARMING

At Gia Mantra we stress at Holistic& Spiritual approach to agriculture.
Our objective is to create farms that functions holistically as one entity with its soil,animals,humans,and plants projected as diverse ‘organs of one living organism “

At Gia Mantra farms the Emphasis is placed on the integration of crops and livestock, recycling of nutrients maintenance of soil, and the health and wellbeing of crops and animals; the farmer too is part of the whole. We incorporate all nine biodynamic preparations for the purpose of enhancing soil quality and stimulating plant life.

We are always keen to welcome visitors, volunteers, and residents to join in our biodynamic revolution.

Come experience biodynamic living! at our beautiful farms from our Expert team through our various training programs & courses and spread love & awareness about sustainable living in your own communities
